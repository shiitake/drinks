---
name: Eva Peron
source: http://imbibemagazine.com/Eva-Peron-Recipe/
ingredients:
   Fernet Branca: 1oz
   Carpano Antica sweet vermouth: 1oz
   ginger liqueur: 1oz
   fresh lime juice: 1oz
   ginger beer: 1oz
---

Shake all but the ginger beer with ice. Strain over fresh ice in a Collins
glass. Top with ginger beer.
